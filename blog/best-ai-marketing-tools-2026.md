## Common Mistakes to Avoid

### **1. Expecting AI to Replace Strategy**
**Mistake:** Letting AI make strategic decisions  
**Solution:** Use AI for execution, humans for strategy

### **2. No Human Oversight**
**Mistake:** Publishing AI content without review  
**Solution:** Always have human editor review

### **3. Ignoring Brand Voice**
**Mistake:** Generic AI content  
**Solution:** Train AI on your brand voice

### **4. Tool Overload**
**Mistake:** Adopting too many tools  
**Solution:** Start with 2-3, expand gradually

### **5. Not Measuring ROI**
**Mistake:** Can't prove value  
**Solution:** Track time saved, revenue impact
