## What is AI Video Generation?

AI video generation uses artificial intelligence to create or edit videos automatically. There are several types:

### **1. Text-to-Video**
Type a description, AI generates a video.  
**Example:** "A cat playing piano in a jazz club" → Full video

### **2. Image-to-Video**
Upload an image, AI animates it.  
**Example:** Static photo → Moving video clip

### **3. AI Video Editing**
AI edits your footage automatically.  
**Example:** Raw footage → Polished video with cuts, transitions, captions

### **4. AI Avatars**
AI-generated presenters for videos.  
**Example:** Script → Video with AI presenter

## Step-by-Step: Creating Your First AI Video

### **Method 1: Text-to-Video (Runway)**

**Step 1:** Sign up for Runway (free tier)

**Step 2:** Click "Gen-2" tool

**Step 3:** Write your prompt:
```
"A golden retriever running through a field of sunflowers at sunset, cinematic, slow motion"
```

**Step 4:** Choose settings:
- Duration: 4 seconds
- Aspect ratio: 16:9
- Style: Cinematic

**Step 5:** Click "Generate"

**Step 6:** Wait 30-60 seconds

**Step 7:** Download your video

**Time:** 2 minutes total  
**Cost:** ~10 credits (free tier)

## Tips for Better AI Videos

### **1. Write Better Prompts**

❌ **Bad:** "A dog"  
✅ **Good:** "A golden retriever puppy playing in a park, sunny day, cinematic lighting, 4K quality"

**Prompt Formula:**
```
[Subject] + [Action] + [Setting] + [Style] + [Quality]
```

---

### **2. Use Reference Images**

Most AI video tools work better with reference images.

**Example:**
- Upload photo of your product
- AI animates it
- Better results than text-only

---

### **3. Keep It Short**

AI video quality decreases with length.

**Recommended:**
- Text-to-video: 4-8 seconds
- AI avatars: 1-3 minutes
- Edited videos: Any length

---

### **4. Combine Tools**

Don't rely on one tool. Combine for best results:

**Example Workflow:**
1. Runway: Generate background video
2. Synthesia: Create avatar overlay
3. Kapwing: Add captions and music
4. Final: Professional video

---

### **5. Add Human Touch**

AI videos work best when combined with human elements:
- Your voice instead of AI voice
- Real footage + AI effects
- Human-written scripts

## Common Mistakes to Avoid

### **Mistake 1: Expecting Perfection**
AI video is impressive but not perfect. Expect some artifacts.

**Solution:** Use AI for 80% of work, polish manually

---

### **Mistake 2: Overusing AI Avatars**
AI avatars can feel impersonal.

**Solution:** Use for training/corporate, not personal branding

---

### **Mistake 3: Ignoring Copyright**
Some AI tools use copyrighted training data.

**Solution:** Use tools with commercial licenses (Synthesia, Pictory)

---

### **Mistake 4: No Editing**
AI output needs refinement.

**Solution:** Always review and edit AI-generated videos

---

### **Mistake 5: Wrong Tool for Job**
Each tool has strengths.

**Solution:** Match tool to use case (see comparison table)
