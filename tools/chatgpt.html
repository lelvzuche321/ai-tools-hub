What is ChatGPT?

ChatGPT is a conversational AI model developed by OpenAI. Launched in November 2022, it has become one of the most popular AI tools worldwide with over 200 million weekly active users. ChatGPT can assist with writing, coding, brainstorming, learning, and much more.

Powered by GPT-4 (in the paid version), ChatGPT uses advanced natural language processing to understand context, generate human-like responses, and help users accomplish a wide variety of tasks. From writing emails to debugging code, ChatGPT has become an indispensable tool for millions of professionals, students, and creators.
